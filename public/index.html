How to Play

The Goal
Place the 7 historical events in chronological order on the timeline. A new puzzle is released every day at midnight (Los Angeles time).

Categories
Puzzle categories rotate daily and include Sports, Landmarks, History, Entertainment, Business, Technology, and Cinema.

Scoring
You start with a Perfect score of 7 out of 7. For every incorrect placement, you lose 1 point. See how high you can score on the daily leaderboard!

Syncing Devices
Want to sync your progress between your phone and your computer?
1. Tap the ⚙ Settings icon on the device with your progress.
2. Copy your Player ID.
3. Open settings on your other device, Paste the ID, and tap Update.
